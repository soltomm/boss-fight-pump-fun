2. PLATFORM DESCRIPTION

2.1 Nature of Service

Alpha Raid is a decentralized gaming platform operating on the Solana blockchain where participants place bets on whether a boss character will be defeated or survive during timed combat phases.

2.2 Game Mechanics

  • Betting Phase: Players place bets (0.1 SOL minimum) on whether the boss will die or survive
  • Fighting Phase: The community collectively damages the boss through interactions
  • Outcome: Winners receive proportional payouts from the total prize pool minus platform fees
  • Boss Health: Boss starts with predetermined health that decreases through player actions
  • Time Limits: Each phase has a countdown timer that determines game progression

2.3 Betting Options

  • Boss Dies: Bet that the boss will be defeated (health reaches 0)
  • Boss Survives: Bet that the boss will retain health when time expires
